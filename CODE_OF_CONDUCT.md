# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in GuardianNode a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the families this project serves
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Unacceptable behavior:
- Sexualized language or imagery, or sexual attention/advances of any kind
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal/political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Posting or linking to actual child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — this will result in a permanent ban and a report to authorities. Do not test our software with real CSAM.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Particular sensitivities

GuardianNode is a child-safety project. We deal with topics including grooming, self-harm, exploitation, and abuse. Issue and PR discussions involving these topics must be:
- Factual and clinical when describing detection signals
- Respectful of survivors
- Free of glorification of harm

If you are a survivor of online abuse and want to contribute, you are welcome here. If discussing a topic is harmful to your wellbeing, please skip those threads.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported through GitHub Issues or Discussions for non-sensitive matters. If the
report includes private information, ask for a private maintainer contact
channel first and do not post child data, screenshots, or evidence publicly. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
